About Us

Elianto Car Dealer is a leading dealer in Indonesia that has been established since 2000. We provide a variety of high quality cars with the best service. Customer trust is our top priority, and we are proud to serve the vehicle needs of the Indonesian people for more than two decades.

Our Vision

"To become a trusted and innovative car dealer that provides high quality mobility solutions for all levels of society, as well as being a leader in automotive services in Indonesia by prioritizing customer satisfaction, sustainability and integrity."

Our Mission

Provide a Superior Customer Experience

Prioritizing the best service with a professional team that is always ready to help customers in choosing a vehicle that suits their needs and budget.

Offering High Quality Vehicles

Only providing cars from trusted brands that have been tested for reliability, safety and efficiency, as well as providing a variety of vehicle choices for all market segments.

Become a Long Term Riding Partner

Providing quality after-sales and service at competitive prices, so that customers feel comfortable and safe in maintaining their vehicles.

Encouraging Innovation and Technology in Services

Using the latest technology in the sales, customer service and after-sales processes to provide convenience, comfort and speed in every transaction.
